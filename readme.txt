HI! Welcome to Dodge wall!

Game information and Instructions : 

-Game starts with an initial score of 50.
-Use up,down, left and right arrow keys to control your triagle and dodge the red and gray squares. 
-Red square results in loss of 1 life and resets the location of your triangle.
-Gray sqaures decreases your score by 75. (SCORE-75)
-You WIN if your score reaches 500  and you have more than 1 life remaining..
-You LOOSE if your score reaches below 0 or you loose all lives.
-Press escape at any time to EXIT the game.
-Press backspace at anytime to restart the game.
